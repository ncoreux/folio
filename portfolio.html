readabl.ai

Data Management Framework, Web

StaySMARTCARE

Remote Patient Monitoring

Specially designed for senior care. Those who prefer to stay home and get the care they need at home. Has a module that automatically communicates with a family member in case of emergency. Stay Healthy is Stay Smart Care's most comprehensive monitoring solution. In addition to the features of Stay Active, it includes health (blood pressure, heart rate, blood oxygen levels, temperature, weight), cognitive (audio analysis) and medication (medications list) monitoring in the system as well as regular communication with the client and their Primary Care Physician.

Client: HealthcareTriangle Inc

Technology: Figma, React, AWS

DataEZ

Data Management Framework, Web

The DataEz framework is highly modular, scalable, HITRUST certified and API driven, where Data ingested in the platform is tested for quality based on customized rules, classified for PHI/PII and tokenized or anonymized. Ingested data is cataloged and its data lineage captured for search and discovery. Data scientists/data engineers can automate the entire process from ingestion to analysis in a single CLICK. This can plug in to various tools like Airflow, Keycloak, Tableau that may be required for data analysis.

Client: HealthcareTriangle Inc

Technology: Figma, React, AWS
